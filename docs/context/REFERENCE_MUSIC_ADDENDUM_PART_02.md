MUSICAL THEMES

Create original recurring themes for:

1. The Estate

A grand but decaying theme representing history, secrecy and institutional power.

2. The Veiled

A concealed, ritualistic theme that can be heard in fragments before the player understands its meaning.

3. The Loyal

A human, fragile theme associated with trust, doubt and loss.

4. The Player

A theme that changes depending on whether the player becomes compassionate, manipulative, feared or isolated.

5. Banishment

A ceremonial theme combining judgement, finality and uncertainty.

6. Nightfall

A darker transformation of the estate theme, introducing danger without immediately becoming action music.

7. Discovery

A restrained investigative motif associated with evidence, contradictions and realisation.

8. Murder

A score built around dread and inevitability rather than only combat intensity.

SILENCE

Silence is an intentional musical tool.

Remove or reduce music during:

- Eavesdropping
- Hiding near guards
- Intimate conversations
- Waiting for a target
- Critical dialogue decisions
- Moments immediately before an elimination
- The discovery of a contradiction

Environmental sound should carry tension when the score withdraws.

- Composed architectural framing
- Elegant camera movement

- Contrasts between luxury and violence
- Strong silhouettes
- Deliberate use of symmetry
- Environmental storytelling
- Visually readable opportunities
- Calm presentation before sudden consequence

- Challenge briefings
- Arrival at the round table
- Vote casting
- Banishment
