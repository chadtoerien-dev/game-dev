# Product Vision

## Product statement

**The Veil is a single-player, third-person social-deduction action RPG in which every physical action can become evidence, every piece of evidence is interpreted through relationships, and every relationship can alter the vote.**

The distinguishing feature is not that social deduction and action appear in the same game. It is that they form one causal simulation:

```text
player action
→ witnesses, traces and memories
→ information spreads with provenance and distortion
→ relationships and suspicions change
→ dialogue, alliances and voting intentions change
→ exile and night-operation opportunities change
→ the next round begins under different conditions
```

## Player fantasy

The player should feel like all four of the following:

1. **Contestant** — build relationships and survive group politics.
2. **Investigator** — reconstruct events and test claims.
3. **Operator** — infiltrate, protect, sabotage or intercept.
4. **Strategist** — decide when to lead, deceive, remain quiet or betray.

## Core design law

No important action should terminate inside its own system.

Examples:

- Entering a bedroom may create a witness, muddy footprints, a moved object or a damaged lock.
- Winning a challenge affects competence, resentment, gratitude and threat perception.
- A lie becomes a persistent claim that may later contradict another statement.
- A vote becomes part of a public political history.

## Product promise for the vertical slice

At the end of one round, the player should be able to say:

- I persuaded someone to trust me.
- Someone changed their mind because of something I did.
- I discovered, concealed or created evidence.
- The group vote was not predetermined.
- My daytime actions materially changed the night.
- The next Dawn Assembly reflected what happened.

## Design pillars

### Trust is a resource

Trust grants information, access, protection, voting support, cooperation, alibis and vulnerability. It can be cultivated, exploited and betrayed.

### Suspicion is systemic and directed

Each NPC has their own opinion of every contestant. Suspicion arises from behaviour, claims, evidence, voting history, associations, withdrawal, aggression, unexplained access, injuries and failed operations.

### Information has provenance

NPCs distinguish personal observation, hearing, report, inference, rumour, physical evidence, trusted sources, unreliable sources and deliberate lies.

### Both factions are active

Veiled play combines manipulation and stealth. Loyal play combines investigation, surveillance, protection, traps, following and interception.

### Action supports the social game

Stealth, sabotage and violence generate social consequences. The night operation is not a separate minigame.

### Multiple personalities are viable

Low-profile, leadership, charm, investigation, manipulation and intimidation should all be valid strategies with meaningful risks.

## Original-IP boundary

The project is inspired by broad social-deduction and systemic-stealth genres but must remain original.

Do not reproduce protected television or game branding, characters, dialogue, music, room designs, missions, UI, costumes, cinematic sequences or other recognisable expression.

Placeholder terms requiring commercial review include:

- Breakfast / Dawn Assembly
- Round Table / Tribunal or Reckoning
- Banishment / Exile
- Murder / Night Strike or Elimination
- Protection / Ward
- Prize Fund / Vault

Faction names **Loyal** and **Veiled** remain provisional.
